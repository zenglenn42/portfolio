About

Introduction

I'm a software creative who enjoys realizing interesting products and applications while challenging others to unlock their potential. My current direction is full-stack web development, though I've worked in realms that range from ground support applications for NASA, device drivers for the Linux kernel, and configuration management for companies like IBM and Zenoss.

Strength in diversity

I work in a variety of settings: public domain, venture-backed, and privately held concerns. In all cases, I cultivate collaborative teams of diverse talent that strive together in a spirit optimism and common purpose. Our impulse to create and maintain perspective even under extraordinary conditions is one of our most noble and humanizing qualities. I value context, proportionality, and discernment. My candle icon signifies a personal choice to confront fear with hope.
